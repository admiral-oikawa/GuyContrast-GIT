Videography

About service

The Approach
and Work Specifics

Engaging cinematic videos curated to fit your needs and requirements. Feature videos, advertisements, documentaries or other video material, any and all of your video needs will be fulfilled by creating a personalized package for you.

View works

Feature Video

+
-

A feature video is a strategic video showcasing the identity and personality of you and your brand. Generally a few minutes long, a feature video portrays the full experience you and your brand aims to provide. In case of products, these can showcase the full capabilities of the product in an engaging way.

Documentary

+
-

A documentary is a non-fictional motion picture intended to document reality, primarily for instruction, education or maintaining a historical record. Documentaries are often used to reveal an unusual, interesting or unknown angle. Documentaries can be a powerful tool for NGOs and non-profit organizations to showcase their work in a natural and unbiased way.

Short-form Content

+
-

Short-form contents are short, attention grabbing, & engaging videos, that are generally published on platforms like TikTok, and Instagram reels. Usually shot vertically, these videos can be the most efficient way to cram a lot of information in a short duration. These are one of the most effective tools used in social media to reach more audience.

Advertisement

+
-

Video advertising is the use of video content to sell products or services. Video advertisements are one of the most effective forms of advertisements, as moving images are easier for customers to recall than static images or text.
